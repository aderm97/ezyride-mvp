Why Ezyride

Built around the way
airports actually work.

  1. 01

    Airport pickups, on your schedule

    Choose your airport and terminal, set your time, and your chauffeur is ready and waiting the moment you land — no guesswork.

  2. 02

    Meet & greet at arrivals

    Your chauffeur waits in the arrivals hall with a name board, helps with luggage, and walks you straight to the car.

  3. 03

    Fixed, all-in pricing

    The price you see at booking is the price you pay. Tolls, waiting time, and airport fees are always included.

  4. 04

    60 minutes of free waiting

    Long immigration queue? Lost bag? Every airport pickup includes a full hour of complimentary waiting after landing.

  5. 05

    Professional chauffeurs

    Every driver is licensed, background-checked, and trained in-house. You'll see their name, photo, and plate before pickup.

  6. 06

    100% electric, always

    Every journey is zero-tailpipe-emission, charged on renewable energy contracts. Premium travel that doesn't cost the sky.

How it works

From booking to boarding
in three steps.

  1. 1 — Book in under a minute

    Enter your route and terminal. Choose a class, see the fixed price, and confirm — no account required.

  2. 2 — We match your chauffeur

    We assign your nearest professional chauffeur and share their details the moment they're on the way to you.

  3. 3 — Step off, step in

    Meet at arrivals or at the kerb — your choice. Sit back in a silent, fully charged EV and let the airport fade behind you.
